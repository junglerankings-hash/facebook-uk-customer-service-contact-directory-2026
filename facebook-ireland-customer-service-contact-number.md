## How to Contact Facebook Ireland (Meta Platforms Ireland Limited)

Facebook Ireland, officially known as Meta Platforms Ireland Limited, serves as the European headquarters for Meta. Located in Dublin, Ireland, this office handles all Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, and Messenger operations for users across Europe, the Middle East, and Africa (EMEA region).

## What Facebook Ireland Handles

Meta Platforms Ireland Limited is the data controller for all Facebook users outside the United States and Canada. This means Facebook Ireland is responsible for:

- Processing personal data for European users under GDPR
- Handling Subject Access Requests (SARs) for EU/EEA residents
- Managing data deletion requests under the Right to Erasure
- Responding to GDPR compliance enquiries
- Processing data portability requests
- Handling complaints related to data protection

## Facebook Ireland GDPR Contact

If you need to make a GDPR request to Facebook Ireland, you can:

1. Use the Facebook Help Centre at facebook.com/help
2. Submit a request through the Facebook Settings > Privacy section
3. Write to the Data Protection Officer at Meta Platforms Ireland Limited
4. Contact the Irish Data Protection Commission if your complaint is not resolved

## How to Report Issues to Facebook Ireland

### Account Recovery
If your Facebook account has been hacked or compromised, you can contact Facebook Ireland support for assistance with account recovery for European users.

### Ad Account Issues
For business advertising account problems, billing disputes, or ad account suspensions affecting European advertisers, Facebook Ireland handles these through their business support channels.

### Content Removal Requests
To request content removal under EU law (including the Digital Services Act), contact Facebook Ireland directly or use the in-app reporting tools.

### Intellectual Property Claims
For copyright or trademark violations affecting European users, submit claims through the Facebook Intellectual Property reporting form or contact Facebook Ireland directly.
